> ## Background
>
> This document outlines the brand voice, tone, and core values of Boats For Sale. It serves as a guide for team members to ensure consistent and effective communication across all platforms and interactions. By adhering to these guidelines, we will build a strong, recognizable brand that resonates with our target audience and fostering trust with our users.
>
> ---
>
> ## Brand Voice
>
> The voice of Boats For Sale should be **confident and knowledgeable, yet approachable and friendly**. It should speak with authority about the boating world and the capabilities of its AI technology, but without being overly technical or intimidating. Think of it as a seasoned boating enthusiast who is also genuinely excited to share their knowledge and help others navigate the process.
>
> ---
>
> ## Brand Tone
>
> The tone will be **positive, enthusiastic, and reassuring**. It should convey a sense of ease and efficiency, reflecting the streamlined nature of the platform.
