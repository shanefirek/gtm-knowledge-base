**Category:** Marketing

## For Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)

- **utm_source:** facebook (or instagram)
- - **utm_medium:** paid_social
  - - **utm_campaign:** brunswick_pilot_july2025
    - - **utm_content:** eclipse_marine (or boateka)
      - - **utm_term:** [boat_model]
       
        - ---

        ## For Google Ads

        - **utm_source:** google
        - - **utm_medium:** cpc
          - - **utm_campaign:** brunswick_pilot_july2025
            - - **utm_content:** eclipse_marine (or boateka)
              - - **utm_term:** [keyword or boat model]
               
                - ---

                ## Sample Final URLs

                ### Meta:
                ```
                yourlandingpage.com?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=brunswick_pilot_july2025&utm_content=eclipse_marine&utm_term=sea_hunt_ultra_234
                ```
