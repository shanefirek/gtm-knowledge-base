# WizzBanger vs NADA: Values to Listing Prices

**Category:** Wizz Banger

## Background

The objective of this report is to determine which service provides more accurate valuations that align better with current market conditions. The methodology involves calculating relative differences between each service's valuation and the listing price, focusing on absolute percentage differences to measure accuracy regardless of over or undervaluation. The document also delves into valuation bias, age-related patterns, boat type-specific observations, and the differing methodologies of WizzBanger and NADA. Ultimately, the report aims to draw conclusions and implications for various market participants (sellers, buyers, insurance, lending, appraisals) and to position each service based on its characteristics.

---

## Analysis

The analysis reveals significant differences in valuation accuracy and market alignment between WizzBanger and NADA.

### Key Findings:

- **Superior Accuracy of WizzBanger:** WizzBanger values are approximately 4.4 times closer to actual market listings than NADA values. WizzBanger shows an average absolute percentage difference of only 4.1% compared to NADA's 18.2%. This indicates WizzBanger is significantly more accurate in reflecting market prices.

- - **WizzBanger's Unbiased Nature:** WizzBanger exhibits a balanced valuation bias, with 5 overvaluations and 5 undervaluations in the sample, indicating no systematic bias. Its average signed percentage difference is nearly zero (-0.03%), suggesting it is unbiased.
 
  - - **NADA's Undervaluation Tendency:** NADA consistently undervalues boats, showing a strong undervaluation tendency with 9 out of 10 boats being undervalued in the sample. Its average signed percentage difference is -15.2%, highlighting this consistent bias.
   
    - - **Tighter Range of Differences for WizzBanger:** WizzBanger's range of differences from listing prices is much tighter, ranging from -9.2% to +9.7%, compared to NADA's wider range of -43.4% to +4.6%. This further underscores WizzBanger's reliability.
     
      - - **Valuation Level Disparity:** WizzBanger values exceed NADA values in 9 out of 10 cases, with an average premium of approximately 35%. The only exception was a newer wake boat, where NADA provided a higher valuation.
       
        - - **Age-Related Patterns:** NADA's undervaluation becomes more pronounced with older boats. For boats from 2002-2012, NADA undervaluations ranged from 23-34%. There's a moderate correlation (~0.3) between boat age and NADA's undervaluation. Newer models (2020+) show closer overall alignment between the two services.
         
          - ### Boat Type Specific Observations:

              - - **Pontoon Boats:** NADA shows extreme undervaluation, specifically -43.4% for the sample pontoon boat.
